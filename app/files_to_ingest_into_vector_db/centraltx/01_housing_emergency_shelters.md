# SECTION 1: HOUSING & EMERGENCY SHELTERS

## Emergency Shelter System Overview

**How City-operated shelter access works (ARCH + Eighth Street Shelter):**
- Shelter placement is managed through a **waitlist overseen by the City of Austin Homeless Strategies and Operations (HSO)**.
- To get on the waitlist, contact the **Sunrise Homeless Navigation Center** at **512-522-1097** (Mon–Fri **8:00 a.m.–8:00 p.m.**).
- HSO closes the waitlist when it reaches **200** clients and plans to reopen it when the list drops to **50** clients (as space becomes available).

**If someone needs immediate help right now:**
- If there is an emergency (medical/safety), call **911**.
- For non-emergency City info and referrals, call **311** (or 512-974-2000 if calling from outside the area).

## Coordinated Entry (Coordinated Assessment) + Housing Programs (Non-shelter)

### ECHO (Ending Community Homelessness Coalition) – Coordinated Entry / Coordinated Assessment

**Website (Get Help / Coordinated Assessment):**
- https://www.austinecho.org/gethelp
- https://www.austinecho.org/leading-system-change/coordinated-entry/

**What it is:**
- ECHO coordinates Austin/Travis County's **Coordinated Entry** system.
- A **Coordinated Assessment (CA)** is the community's centralized housing-assistance application used by many housing programs.

**Who can do a CA (high-level):**
- People currently experiencing homelessness in Austin/Travis County (including living in an emergency shelter, in a place not meant for habitation like a tent/car, or fleeing/attempting to flee domestic violence).
- Within 90 days of jail/prison release if homeless beforehand.
- Note: Those in unstable situations (couch surfing, renting rooms) typically don't qualify.

**How to do a CA – Phone Assessment:**
- Sunrise Homeless Navigation Center: 512-522-1097 (Mon–Fri, 8am–8pm)
- The SAFE Alliance: 512-267-7233 (by appointment)

**How to do a CA – In-Person Drop-In Locations:**
| Location | Address | Hours |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Sunrise Navigation Center | 4430 Menchaca Rd | Mon–Fri 9am–1pm |
| Endeavors (ARCH) | 500 E 7th St | Mon–Fri 8am–4:30pm |
| Downtown Austin Community Court | 505 Barton Springs Rd | Tuesdays 10am–2pm |
| The Charlie Center | 12675 Research Blvd | Wed–Thu 10am–1pm |
| Austin Area Urban League | 8011 Cameron Rd | Tue & Fri 10am–1pm |

**How to do a CA – Mobile Outreach (providers visit you):**
- Sunrise Navigation Center: Online referral form at sunrisenavigationcenter.org
- Endeavors (formerly Urban Alchemy): Contact through ARCH at 512-881-8951
- HOST: 512-804-3720
- PATH/Integral Care: 512-804-3700
- Heart of Texas Outreach: 512-522-4316

**Important notes:**
- Completing a CA is **not a guarantee** of housing; it can take months or longer to be contacted by a program.
- Assessment takes under one hour.
- Language services available (200+ languages via interpreter line).
- No immigration status inquiry.
- Reassessment recommended after 6 months without program contact.

**Source URLs (verified January 15, 2026):**

### Community First! Village (Mobile Loaves & Fishes) – Permanent Housing (Austin)

**Who it's for (eligibility – high level):**
- Mobile Loaves & Fishes states applicants must be **chronically homeless** (as defined by HUD) and have at least one disability
- Additional eligibility requirements include: verifiable income (minimum $600–$825/month depending on housing type), age 18+, residency in **Travis, Williamson, Hays, Caldwell, or Bastrop counties** (for at least the past year), and meeting criminal history requirements
- Note: Applicants on the Sex Offender Registry will be automatically denied

**How to apply / get started:**
1. Complete a Coordinated Assessment (CA)
2. Attend a “Potential Neighbor” tour (currently listed as virtual; registration required)
3. Complete the housing application (sent after the tour)

**Wait time note:**
- MLF notes the time between application submission and approval can be **up to 1 year or more**.

**Contact / address:**
- Housing hotline: (512) 551-5471  
- Email: housing@mlf.org  
- Address (MLF): 9116 Hog Eye Road, Building 3405, Austin, TX 78724
