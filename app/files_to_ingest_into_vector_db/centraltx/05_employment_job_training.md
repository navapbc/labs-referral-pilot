- Tuition, books, and fees covered for selected in-demand career pathways (program model and partner schools vary)

3. Upload requested documents
4. Take assessment and attend career guidance meeting

### Austin Community College (ACC) Career Services

**Phone:** (512) 223-1470
**Email:** career.services@austincc.edu (students) | employer.services@austincc.edu (employers)
**Website:** https://students.austincc.edu/career-services/
**Main Location:** Highland Campus, 6101 Highland Campus Drive, Austin, TX 78752
**Additional Career Make It Centers:** Round Rock Campus, Riverside Campus

**Hours:**
- Mon–Thu 8:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m., Fri 8:00 a.m.–12:00 p.m. (noon)
- Walk-ins: Wednesdays at Highland Campus (no appointment needed)

**What They Offer (free):**
- One-on-one career coaching and career counseling (licensed counselors available)
- Resume and cover letter review/writing assistance
- Interview preparation and mock interviews (including "Big Interview" AI platform)
- Job and internship search via Handshake job board
- LinkedIn and Indeed profile help, personal branding, salary negotiation
- Weekly workshops (Job Search 101, Interview Skills, Resume Development)
- Job fairs and employer networking events
- **"Strategies for Today's Jobs"** — a free 4-week job search course offered monthly, **open to the general public** (not just ACC students)

**Eligibility:**
- **Current ACC students:** Full access to all services
- **ACC alumni:** Can access Handshake job board and connect with Career Services
- **General public / community members:** The "Strategies for Today's Jobs" course is free and open to all job seekers (registration through ACC's system required — new participants must create an ACC account)

**How to Access:**
- By appointment: Submit request through the Career Services online appointment form on their website
- Walk-in: Every Wednesday at Highland Campus Career and Transfer Center
- Online: Many services available remotely; workshops offered in virtual formats

**Source URLs (verified February 9, 2026):**
- https://students.austincc.edu/career-services/
- https://students.austincc.edu/career-services/strategies-for-todays-jobs/

### City of Austin First Workers' Day Labor Program

**Address:** 700 East 7th Street, Austin, TX 78701
**Phone:** (512) 972-4100
**Website:** https://www.austintexas.gov/department/day-labor

> **Note:** The program has indicated it is in the process of relocating. Call (512) 972-4100 to confirm the current location before visiting.

**Hours:**
- Walk-in: Mon–Fri, 5:30 a.m.–11:00 a.m.
- Call center: Mon–Fri, 5:30 a.m.–2:00 p.m.

**What They Offer:**
- Same-day cash-paid job placements (carpentry, painting, roofing, plumbing, landscaping, welding, demolition, general labor)
- Moral Reconation Therapy (MRT) — a four-week class series for re-entry clients (anger management, cognitive skills, job readiness)
- Case management referrals, community nursing access, food assistance, and rent/utility assistance referrals

**Who It's For:**
- Individuals experiencing homelessness
- Migrant workers
- Re-entry individuals (returning from incarceration)
- Anyone seeking day labor work

**How to Access:**
1. Walk in to the Day Labor Center during office hours (5:30–11:00 a.m.)
2. Call (512) 972-4100 during call center hours

**Source URLs (verified February 5, 2026):**
- https://www.austintexas.gov/department/day-labor
