# SECTION 2: MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES

## Local Mental Health Authorities (LMHAs)

### Integral Care (Travis County LMHA)

**24/7 Crisis Helpline:** (512) 472-HELP (4357)  
**Toll-Free:** (844) 398-8252  
**TTY:** (512) 703-1395  
**Website:** https://integralcare.org  
**Email:** info@integralcare.org  

**Walk-in psychiatric urgent care (24/7):**  
**Psychiatric Emergency Services (PES) – Richard E. Hopkins Behavioral Health Building**  
- Address: 1165 Airport Blvd., Second Floor, Austin, TX 78702  
- Phone: (512) 472-4357  
- Hours: **Open 24 hours, 7 days a week**

**How to access services:**
1. Call the **24/7 Crisis Helpline** for immediate support and connection to programs/services
2. For **non-crisis** questions about programs/services, call the helpline during **Mon–Fri 8:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m.** (except holidays) and follow the phone prompts
3. If you need urgent in-person support, **walk in** to PES (open 24/7). If you are in immediate danger, call **911**

**Source URLs (verified January 15, 2026):**
- https://integralcare.org
- https://integralcare.org/program/psychiatric-emergency-services-pes/
