# SECTION 11: DIAPER BANKS & BABY SUPPLIES

### Austin Diaper Bank
**What they do:**
- Provides diapers, wipes, and period products through **partner agencies** across Central Texas (not direct-to-individual pickup).
- Partners with 40 agencies across 7 counties, serving 37,000+ families monthly

**How to get diapers (recommended):**
1. Use the "Find Diapers" tool / partner agency list to locate an agency near you (enter your zip code).
2. Contact the agency for their hours and distribution process.

**Phone:** (512) 710-7232
**Email:** info@austindiapers.org
**Address:** 2210 Denton Drive, Suite 103, Austin, TX 78758
**Website:** https://austindiapers.org/get-help/
**Helpline:** Call 2-1-1 or visit www.211texas.org

**Products available through partner agencies:**
- Baby diapers (newborn through size 6)
- Pull-Ups
- Baby wipes
- Period products

**Source URLs (verified January 30, 2026):**
- https://austindiapers.org/get-help/
- https://austindiapers.org/contact-us/

### Texas WIC (Women, Infants, and Children)
**What it is:**  
- Nutrition support for pregnant people, postpartum/breastfeeding people, infants, and children under age 5.

**How to apply / find a clinic:**  
- Use the Texas WIC site to apply and locate a local WIC office.

**Phone:** 1-800-942-3678  
**Website:** https://texaswic.org/

**Source URLs (verified January 15, 2026):**
