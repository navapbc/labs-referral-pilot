### The Trevor Project (24/7 LGBTQ+ crisis support)
- **Call:** 1-866-488-7386
- **Text:** "START" to 678678
- **Chat:** Available via website
- **Hours:** Available **24/7, 365 days a year**

> **Note (July 2025):** The Trump administration terminated the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline's LGBTQ+ Youth Specialized Services program. However, **The Trevor Project's own crisis lines remain fully operational 24/7** and are not affected by this change.

**Source URLs (verified January 30, 2026):**

### Trans Lifeline
- **U.S.:** 877-565-8860
- **Canada:** 877-330-6366
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 10:00 a.m.–6:00 p.m. Pacific Time (1:00 p.m.–9:00 p.m. Eastern)
- **Note:** Trans Lifeline reduced hours in 2024 due to funding challenges. For Spanish support, press #2 when calling.

> **Important:** Trans Lifeline is **no longer available 24/7**. If you need immediate crisis support outside their operating hours, contact **The Trevor Project** (1-866-488-7386, available 24/7) or the **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** (call or text 988).
