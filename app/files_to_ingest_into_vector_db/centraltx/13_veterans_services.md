## National Call Center for Homeless Veterans (VA)
**Phone:** 1-877-4AID-VET (877-424-3838)  
**Available:** 24/7  
**What it’s for:** Homeless veterans (or veterans at risk) and people supporting them; connects callers to VA homeless programs and local resources.

**Source URLs (verified January 15, 2026):**

## SSVF (Supportive Services for Veteran Families)
**What it is:** A VA program that funds local providers to help very low-income veterans and their families avoid homelessness or quickly return to stable housing (rapid rehousing + homelessness prevention).

**Source URLs (verified January 15, 2026):**
- https://www.va.gov/homeless/ssvf/ssvf-overview/
