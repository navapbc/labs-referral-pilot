## Legal Services – Expungement and Record Sealing

### Pennsylvania Clean Slate Law

Website: https://nationalreentryresourcecenter.org/cleanslate/states/pennsylvania

What it is:
- Pennsylvania law that automatically seals certain criminal records.
- Improves access to jobs, housing, and education.

Eligibility for automatic sealing:
- Summary convictions: After 10 years with no subsequent convictions
- Misdemeanors (grade 2 or 3) resulting in 2 years or less: After 10 years arrest-free
- Non-convictions: Automatically sealed

Petition-based sealing:
- Misdemeanors: May petition 10 years after conviction if no subsequent convictions
- Summary offenses: May be expunged 5 years after conviction if no arrests

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://nationalreentryresourcecenter.org/cleanslate/states/pennsylvania

## Tips for Successful Reentry

1. Start planning early – Contact PA CareerLink or reentry organizations before release if possible
2. Get your vital documents – Birth certificate, Social Security card, and ID are essential
3. Be honest but strategic – Many employers are willing to hire; focus on skills and growth
4. Use fair chance resources – Look for second chance employers
5. Build a support network – Connect with peer support, mentors, or faith communities
6. Address underlying issues – Substance use treatment and mental health support improve outcomes
7. Know your rights – Some records can be expunged or sealed; consult legal aid
