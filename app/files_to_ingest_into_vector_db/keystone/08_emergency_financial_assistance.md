## Pennsylvania Cash Assistance Programs

### TANF (Temporary Assistance for Needy Families)

Website: https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dhs/resources/cash-assistance/tanf
Apply Online: https://www.compass.state.pa.us
Phone (Welfare Helpline): 1-800-692-7462

What it is:
- Monthly cash assistance for families with low income.
- Helps pay for housing, food, and other necessary expenses.

Time limits:
- 60 months (5 years) total over lifetime
- TANF "Time-Out" exceptions may apply (up to 12 months that don't count toward limit)
- Extended TANF available in certain circumstances

Eligibility requirements:
- U.S. citizen or eligible non-citizen
- Pennsylvania resident
- Resources (bank accounts, bonds, property other than primary residence) must be $1,000 or less
- Child under 18 (or 18 and full-time student) living with you
- Required to look for job or participate in employment/training (exceptions for disability)
- Need support due to: death of parent, incapacity of parent, continued absence of parent, or low/no income from parents' employment

Extended TANF eligibility:
- Working or in approved training
- Victim of domestic violence
- Physical or mental condition preventing work
- Family crisis
- Caring for disabled individual

How to apply:
1. Online at www.compass.state.pa.us
2. Paper application at County Assistance Office
3. Call 1-800-692-7462 for help

Additional support:
- Child care assistance
- Transportation assistance
- Work-related expenses

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dhs/resources/cash-assistance/tanf
- https://www.pa.gov/services/dhs/apply-for-temporary-assistance-for-needy-families-tanf

Website: https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dhs/resources/cash-assistance
Apply: Through COMPASS or County Assistance Office

What it is:
- Limited cash assistance for individuals without children who don't qualify for TANF.
- Pennsylvania state-funded program (not federal).

How to apply:
- Apply through COMPASS or at your County Assistance Office
