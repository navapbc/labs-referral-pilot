## Healthy Steps Diaper Bank

Phone: 717-919-2590
Website: https://healthystepsdiaperbank.com
Email: Contact through website

What it is:
- The only diaper bank in South Central Pennsylvania.
- Founded in 2013, has provided over 4 million diapers to local families.
- Partners with approximately 37 family service agencies.

Counties served:
- Dauphin County
- Cumberland County
- Perry County
- Lancaster County
- York County
- Franklin County

Products available through partner agencies:
- Baby diapers (various sizes)
- Wipes
- Period products

How to access:
- Diapers are distributed through partner agencies (not direct pickup)
- Contact a partner agency in your area
- Partner agencies include women's shelters and family service agencies
- Case managers at partner agencies order diapers for their clients

How it works:
1. Contact a family service agency in your county
2. Work with a case manager who identifies your diaper needs
3. Receive diapers through the partner agency
4. Reduces transportation barriers by combining services

Note: Healthy Steps Diaper Bank does not distribute directly to individuals. Contact PA 211 (dial 211) for help finding a partner agency near you.

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://healthystepsdiaperbank.com
- https://www.tfec.org/featured/nonprofit-organization-healthy-steps-diaper-bank/

## Pennsylvania WIC (Women, Infants & Children)

WIC Hotline: 1-800-WIC-WINS (1-800-942-9467)
Apply Online: https://www.pawic.com/OnlineApplication.aspx
Website: https://www.pawic.com

What it is:
- Nutrition program for pregnant women, new mothers, infants, and children under 5.
- Does NOT provide diapers, but provides formula, food, and nutrition support.

Who can apply:
- Pregnant women
- Breastfeeding parents (up to 12 months after birth)
- Postpartum parents (up to 6 months after birth)
- Infants and children up to age 5

Benefits include:
- Healthy food on a WIC card
- Infant formula
- Nutrition education
- Breastfeeding support
- Referrals to community services

Note: WIC cannot be used for diapers. See diaper bank resources above.

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.pawic.com

## Important Information

- 1 in 2 families with young children struggle to afford diapers
- Average diaper cost: $80-$100+ per month per baby
- Neither WIC nor SNAP can be used to purchase diapers
- Many community agencies can help connect you with diaper assistance

If you need diapers or baby supplies:
1. Call PA 211 (dial 211) for local resources
2. Contact Healthy Steps Diaper Bank partner agencies
3. Reach out to local pregnancy resource centers
4. Ask about baby supplies when accessing other services (food pantries, emergency assistance)
