## LGBT Center of Central PA

Address: 1306 North 3rd Street, Harrisburg, PA 17102, Dauphin County
Phone: 717-409-5781
Website: https://centralpalgbtcenter.org
Email: Contact through website

Hours:
- Monday–Friday: 10:00 am – 4:00 pm

What it is:
- The only LGBTQ+ community center in Dauphin County.
- Founded in 2006.
- Serves over 2,000 people annually.
- Creates affirming spaces for LGBTQ+ people to find community, connection, and support.

Counties served:
- Dauphin
- Cumberland
- Perry
- Lancaster
- York

Services:
- Emergency and transitional housing for queer and trans people facing housing insecurity
- Food bank (feeds over 100 individuals monthly)
- Support groups
- Educational programs
- Social and cultural programs
- Resource and referral information
- Drop-in center
- Community outreach

Programs:

Common Roads (Youth and Young Adults):
- Ages 12-17: Youth programs
- Ages 18-35: Young adult programs
- Social and educational programming
- Safe space for LGBTQ+ youth

GLO Harrisburg (QTPOC):
- Community resource center for queer and trans people of color
- Free and confidential STI/HIV testing
- Virtual therapy
- Access to PrEP
- HIV care linkage
- Social services

How to access:
- Walk in during office hours
- Call 717-409-5781
- Visit website for current programs

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://centralpalgbtcenter.org
- https://centralpalgbtcenter.org/commonroads

## Crisis and Support Hotlines

### The Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth Crisis)

Phone: 1-866-488-7386 (24/7)
Text: Text "START" to 678-678
Chat: Available at www.thetrevorproject.org
Website: https://www.thetrevorproject.org/get-help/

What it is:
- 24/7 crisis support for LGBTQ+ young people.
- Mission to end suicide among LGBTQ+ youth.
- Counselors listen without judgment.
- All conversations confidential.

Who it's for:
- LGBTQ+ young people in crisis
- Those who need someone to talk to

How to access:
- Call 1-866-488-7386 (24/7)
- Text "START" to 678-678
- Chat online at thetrevorproject.org

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.thetrevorproject.org/get-help/
