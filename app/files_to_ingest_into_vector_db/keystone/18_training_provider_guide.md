## Funding Your Training: Key Programs

### WIOA (Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act) Scholarships

Through PA CareerLink, individuals may be eligible for training scholarships covering:
- Healthcare (CNA, Medical Assistant, Phlebotomy)
- Information Technology (CompTIA certifications)
- Skilled Trades (HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, Welding)
- Transportation (CDL)

**To Apply:** Visit your local PA CareerLink office

### Financial Aid (for longer programs)

* **FAFSA:** studentaid.gov – for associate degrees and eligible certificate programs
* **Pell Grants:** Do not need to be repaid
* **Stevens Grant:** Covers tuition, meals, housing at Thaddeus Stevens for Pell-eligible students

### Trade Adjustment Assistance (TAA)

For workers who lost jobs due to foreign trade. Contact PA CareerLink.
