## Office of Vocational Rehabilitation (OVR)

### Pennsylvania OVR – Statewide

Website: https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dli/programs-services/disability-and-vocational-rehabilitation/vocational-rehabilitation
Apply Online: https://www.pa.gov/services/dli/apply-for-vocational-rehabilitation-services
Central Office Address: 651 Boas Street, 7th Floor, Harrisburg, PA 17121, Dauphin County
Phone: 717-787-5244 (Voice)
TTY: 717-787-4885
Toll-Free: 1-800-442-6351 (Voice) | 1-866-830-7327 (TTY)

What it is:
- State agency providing vocational rehabilitation services to help persons with disabilities prepare for, obtain, or maintain employment.
- Services provided individually through OVR counselors and a network of approved vendors.
- 21 District Offices statewide serving all 67 counties.

Services include:
- Vocational guidance and counseling (no cost)
- Diagnostic services and vocational evaluation
- Training (academic, vocational/technical, college, on-the-job)
- Job placement assistance
- Assistive technology devices and services
- Independent living skills training
- Personal services (readers, sign language interpreters)

Cost:
- Counseling, diagnostic services, job placement, and personal services are provided at no cost
- Social Security disability beneficiaries are exempt from financial needs test
- Some services may be subject to financial participation based on income

Eligibility:
- Individuals with a physical or mental impairment that results in a substantial impediment to employment
- Requires VR services to prepare for, secure, retain, or regain employment
- Able to benefit from VR services in terms of employment outcome

How to apply:
1. Contact your local OVR District Office
2. Apply online through PA CareerLink
3. OVR will contact you within 10 days of referral

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dli/programs-services/disability-and-vocational-rehabilitation/vocational-rehabilitation
- https://www.pa.gov/services/dli/apply-for-vocational-rehabilitation-services

## Unemployment Compensation

### Pennsylvania Unemployment Compensation

Website: https://www.uc.pa.gov
Phone: 1-888-313-7284
Apply Online: https://www.uc.pa.gov/unemployment-benefits/file/Pages/File%20an%20Initial%20Claim.aspx

What it is:
- Temporary income for workers who have lost their job through no fault of their own.

Eligibility:
- Must have worked and earned wages in PA
- Must be able and available to work
- Must actively seek work

How to apply:
1. File online at www.uc.pa.gov
2. Create an account and submit your claim
3. Continue filing bi-weekly claims while unemployed
