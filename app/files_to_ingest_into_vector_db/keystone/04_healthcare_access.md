# SECTION 4: HEALTHCARE ACCESS

## Pennsylvania Medical Assistance (Medicaid)

### COMPASS – Online Benefits Application

Website: https://www.compass.state.pa.us
Track Application: https://trackmybenefits.pa.gov
Phone (Health Care Coverage): 1-866-550-4355
Phone (General DHS Questions): 1-800-692-7462

What it is:
- COMPASS is Pennsylvania's online tool to apply for health and human service programs including Medical Assistance (Medicaid), SNAP, cash assistance, and LIHEAP.

Programs available through COMPASS:
- Medical Assistance (Medicaid)
- CHIP (Children's Health Insurance Program)
- SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program)
- Cash Assistance (TANF)
- LIHEAP (Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program)

Eligibility (2025 income limits):
- Adults (19-64): Up to 138% of Federal Poverty Level (FPL)
- Pregnant women: Up to 220% FPL
- Children: Up to 319% FPL (Medicaid or CHIP)
- Seniors/disabled: Various programs (Healthy Horizons, QMB/SLMB)

Documents needed:
- Proof of identity (PA driver's license, PA ID card, or passport)
- Social Security Number for each applicant
- Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, child support documentation)
- Proof of Pennsylvania residency
- Bank account and asset information (for some programs)
- Immigration documentation (if applicable)

How to apply:
1. Online at https://www.compass.state.pa.us
2. By phone at 1-866-550-4355
3. In person at your County Assistance Office
4. By mail using PA 600 application form

Processing time:
- Usually about 30 days

Renewals:
- Watch for a large PINK envelope in the mail
- Submit renewal online via COMPASS or to your local CAO by the due date

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.compass.state.pa.us
- https://www.pa.gov/services/dhs/apply-for-medicaid-benefits
- https://www.healthinsurance.org/medicaid/pennsylvania/

## Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs)

**Key FQHC Features:**
- Cannot deny services due to inability to pay
- Sliding fee scale based on income and family size
- Accept Medicare, Medicaid, and most insurance
- Offer assistance with coverage enrollment (varies by clinic)

### PA PACE/PACENET (Seniors 65+)

Phone: 1-800-225-7223
Website: https://www.aging.pa.gov/aging-services/prescriptions/Pages/default.aspx

What it is:
- State pharmaceutical assistance programs for Pennsylvania residents 65+.

PACE:
- For seniors 65+ with income up to $14,500 (single) or $17,700 (married)
- Pay $6 for generic, $9 for brand name drugs

PACENET:
- For seniors 65+ with income $14,500-$27,500 (single) or $17,700-$35,500 (married)
- Pay $8 for generic, $15 for brand name drugs (after deductible)

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.aging.pa.gov/aging-services/prescriptions/Pages/default.aspx
