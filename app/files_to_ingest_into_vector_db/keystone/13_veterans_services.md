## Crisis and Homeless Veteran Resources

### Veterans Crisis Line

Phone: Dial **988**, then press **1**
Alternate Phone: 1-800-273-8255 (Press 1)
Text: 838255
Chat: https://www.veteranscrisisline.net
Website: https://www.veteranscrisisline.net

What it is:
- 24/7 confidential crisis support for veterans.
- Connects to VA resources and support.
- Since July 2022, veterans can reach the Crisis Line by dialing 988 and pressing 1.

How to access:
- Call 988, then press 1 (preferred)
- Or call 1-800-273-8255, press 1
- Text 838255
- Chat online

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.veteranscrisisline.net

---

### National Call Center for Homeless Veterans

Phone: 1-877-4AID-VET (1-877-424-3838)
Available: 24/7
Website: https://www.va.gov/HOMELESS/NationalCallCenter.asp

What it is:
- 24/7 hotline for homeless veterans or veterans at risk of homelessness.
- Connects callers to VA homeless programs and local resources.

Who it's for:
- Homeless veterans
- Veterans at risk of homelessness
- People supporting homeless veterans

How to access:
- Call 1-877-424-3838 (24/7)
- Trained counselors ready to help

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.va.gov/HOMELESS/NationalCallCenter.asp

## SSVF (Supportive Services for Veteran Families)

### VMC Center – Harrisburg Office

Address: 21 South 3rd Street, Harrisburg, PA 17101, Dauphin County
Phone: 844-226-0368 (toll-free)
Email: CentralPAInfo@VMCenter.org
Website: https://www.vmcenter.org/services/supportive-services-for-veterans-and-families-ssvf/

What it is:
- VA-funded program helping low-income veterans avoid homelessness or return to stable housing.
- No cost to access programs.

Counties served:
- Dauphin
- Lebanon
- Lancaster
- Schuylkill
- Berks
- Northumberland
- Juniata
- Snyder
- Union
- And additional Central PA counties

Eligibility:
- Veteran with discharge other than dishonorable
- At least one day of active duty service
- Very low income
- Currently homeless or at risk of homelessness

Services:
- Housing counseling
- Case management
- Financial assistance (rent, utilities, security deposits)
- Legal support
- Credit repair
- Financial planning
- Advocacy
- Help obtaining VA benefits
- Help obtaining needed documents

How to access:
- Call 844-226-0368
- Email CentralPAInfo@VMCenter.org
- Walk in to Harrisburg office

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.vmcenter.org/services/supportive-services-for-veterans-and-families-ssvf/
