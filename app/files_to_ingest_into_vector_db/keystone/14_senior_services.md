## PACE and PACENET (Prescription Assistance)

Phone: 1-800-225-7223
Website: https://www.pa.gov/agencies/aging/aging-programs-and-services/pace-program
Email: papace@primetherapeutics.com
Mail: PACE/PACENET, PO Box 8806, Harrisburg, PA 17105-8806

What it is:
- State programs helping qualified residents 65+ get prescription medications at lower cost.
- Works with Medicare Part D to reduce out-of-pocket costs.
- Serves over 250,000 older Pennsylvanians.
- Funded by Pennsylvania Lottery.

PACE Program:
- For single persons with income up to $14,500/year
- For married persons with combined income up to $17,700/year
- Co-payments: $6 generic, $9 brand name (30-day supply)
- No enrollment fee

PACENET Program:
- For single persons with income $14,501-$33,500/year
- For married persons with combined income $17,701-$41,500/year
- Co-payments: $8 generic, $15 brand name (30-day supply)
- Monthly fee: $41.16 (waived if enrolled in Medicare Part D)

Eligibility:
- 65 years of age or older
- Pennsylvania resident for at least 90 days
- Not enrolled in Medicaid prescription benefit

How to apply:
1. Call 1-800-225-7223 (have income and insurance info ready)
2. Apply online through PA Department of Aging
3. Mail paper application

Source URLs (verified January 2026):
- https://www.pa.gov/agencies/aging/aging-programs-and-services/pace-program
- https://www.pa.gov/services/aging/apply-for-the-pharmaceutical-assistance-contract-for-the-elderly
